Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2024

Vintage College Shop is a demo storefront created for coursework. We value your privacy and only use information you share to demonstrate user flows within this project.

Information we collect

We collect the name, email address, and message you submit through the contact form. This data is stored locally in your browser to simulate a support inbox.

How information is used

  • To respond to your inquiries or styling requests.
  • To improve the UX of this student project based on anonymized analytics.
  • To demonstrate e-commerce patterns in an academic setting.

Cookies & local storage

Your cart and theme preference are stored in your browser using local storage. No third-party trackers are installed.

Data sharing

We do not sell or share your data. When collaborating on coursework, only aggregated insights are discussed.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email privacy@vintagecollegeshop.com.
